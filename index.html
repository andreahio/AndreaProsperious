Ecological
Orientation

Ecological orientation is a philosophy of mind which takes cognition to be an activity present at all scales of life. In essence, anything that has a trajectory that can be measured as time and space can be said to have a position that is ecological because it has movement that can be measured as spatial or temporal relative to other positions.
